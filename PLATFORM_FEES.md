## üéØ Stripe's Fee Structure

### **Standard Pricing (US):**
- **Domestic Cards:** 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- **International Cards:** 3.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- **Currency Conversion:** Additional 1% fee

### **Stripe Connect (when transferring to sellers):**
- **Transfer Fee:** $0 (included in standard pricing)
- **Account Fees:** $2/month per active connected account (optional)

### **What Stripe Charges For:**
- Payment processing
- Fraud detection
- PCI compliance
- Dispute handling
- International transactions
- Currency conversion
